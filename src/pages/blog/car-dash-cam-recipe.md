<p>This recipe inspired by car dash camera on my car. When I was playing with the camera, I saw image from dash cam looks green and it&#8217;s appealing. So, I tried to replicated that tones on the spot and got the picture in the car park.</p>



<p>This recipe works at night/dark place. Gives yellow to green tone. Add maximum grain to make it is a picture come from cheap car dash camera.</p>



<p>I tried with using this film simulation on daylight. It&#8217;s give me glimpse of old image that looks yellow. Give it a try! I personally found it&#8217;s nice to underexpose a bit.</p>
